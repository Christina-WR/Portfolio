Serendipitous Discovery

Capstone Project Sponsored by PitchBook Data


The Project

PitchBook Data is an online data platform that provides research, financial information, and resources to analyze private companies, investments and funds, private equity and venture capital. PitchBook's goal was to explore new ways to surface insightful information to their users by allowing them to discover more information.


My Role: UX Designer & Researcher

  • Interviewed stakeholders and potential users
  • Analyzed qualitative data from interviews
  • Created wireframes in Axure
  • Conducted usability tests
  • Created high fidelity interactive prototypes in Axure, primarily focusing on Basic Search and Advanced Search


The Process

Research

  • Persona Analysis
  • Domain Research
  • Analysis of Existing Structure
  • Informational Interviews
  • Competitive Analysis

Design Method

Three rounds of user testing were performed throughout the design process to test navigational components, validate content, information validation and user acceptance testing. Axure was used for wireframes and prototypes.
