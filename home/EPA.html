Beautique

Experience & Service Design Project


Problem

Trying new haircuts can be challenging and intimidating. People are often unsure what hairstyles would flatter their face shape, follow current trends, and would be within their skill-level to style daily.

Objective

Created a service to improve the overall salon experience and reduce anxiety for people trying a new hairstyle.


My Role: Project Manager, UX Designer

  • Scheduled meetings, determined timelines
  • Interviewed stakeholders
  • Brainstormed and created customer stories
  • Created customer journey map
  • Designed paper prototype
